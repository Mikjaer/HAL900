Connections:
2x Wiegand cable + Bolt cable: 3x RJ45

3x GPIO per RJ45 connector + 1 common alarm => 3x4 + 1 = 10 GPIO

Common: PC3
KBD port:  PC0, PC1, PD2
RFID port: PC2, PC3, PD4
Lock:      PD6, PB0, PD7
!Exit:     PB7

PB1: Relay/LED 1
PD5: Relay/LED 2
